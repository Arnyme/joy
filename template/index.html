About TAYCO
TANZANIA YOUTH CENTRE ORGANIZATION (TAYCO)
is a registered Non-Governmental Organization. Has this 08-12-2023 been duly registered under the Non-governmental Organizations Act, 2002 with Registration No ooNGO/R/5477. The Organization will operate at National level.

History of Mr Kelvin Cletus Kagoma in evolution of TAYCO
is the original founder and Chief Executive Director of the Tanzania Youth Centre Organization (TAYCO). In 2022, he was elected as the chairman of TAYCO and successfully led and managed all the organization's activities, even before it was officially registered with the relevant ministry. He oversaw the registration process of the organization despite numerous challenges, until it was finally registered in December 8, 2023. After registration, he was elected as the first Chief Executive Director to lead TAYCO at the national level.

His leadership journey began at a young age, where he was prioritized by his peers to lead them in sports and other activities, detesting bullying, injustice, and other problems, and always being eager to resolve conflicts among others. His parents recognized this early on, as did his teachers at Mji Mpya Primary School, where he received his primary education, and they encouraged him to excel and achieve his dreams for the benefit of the nation. In 2016, while in Form Two, he was elected as the head prefect at Halisi Secondary School in Dar es Salaam, where he successfully led for two terms until 2018. He joined the Youth of United Nations Associations and the Tanzania Red Cross Society in 2019 at the Institute of Finance Management, participating in various activities at both the institutional and national levels. In 2023, he was elected as the chairman of the United Nations Chapter at IFM. Mr. Kelvin Cletus Kagoma had long harbored the idea of establishing an organization that would serve as a one-stop center for all Tanzanian youth, although various challenges hindered him from introducing it to people in different places. Many youth embraced the idea but found it difficult to join because they doubted its feasibility due to its magnitude.

It took him many years until 2020 when he began to assemble a strong and highly cooperative team to establish the TANZANIA YOUTH CENTRE ORGANIZATION, although they faced numerous challenges in reaching their goal. He believes that through TAYCO, they will be able to take a forefront role in identifying and addressing the problems faced by youth and the nation as a whole. TAYCO will also focus on conducting research and programs to identify opportunities for youth and empower them to utilize these opportunities for sustainable development for themselves and the nation. Additionally, through TAYCO, they will engage in activities aimed at protecting our planet for our benefit and that of future generations.

Mr. KELVIN CLETUS KAGOMA continues to remind and urge all young people who are crucial components of the nation that you always become what you feed your mind, so you must choose the right things to feed your mind.
